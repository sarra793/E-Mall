Welcome to Palestine Portfolio

This website honors the culture, struggle, and resilience of the Palestinian people.

About Palestine

  • Rich culture, hospitality, and resistance
  • One of the oldest continuously inhabited regions on Earth
  • Home to ancient cities like Jericho and Hebron
  • Rich in religious significance and spiritual heritage
  • People known for hospitality, strength, and community values
  • Traditional crafts like embroidery (tatreez) and olivewood carving are still practiced today
  • Famous dishes include Maqluba, Musakhan, and Kanafeh
  • Poetry and storytelling are powerful forms of expression and memory
  • For decades, the Palestinian people have lived under occupation, deprived of fundamental rights, subjected to forced displacement, and faced with severe restrictions on their freedom of movement, expression, and daily life.
  • Cities like Gaza regularly endure suffocating blockades, power outages, limited access to clean water, and infrastructure destroyed by repeated bombings.
  • In the West Bank, checkpoints, home demolitions, the expansion of illegal settlements, and arbitrary arrests make daily life extremely difficult.
  • Despite these conditions, Palestinians continue to resist peacefully, defending their identity, their culture, and their right to live freely on their land.
  • Children go to school amid the rubble, families cultivate their land under surveillance, and artists turn pain into poetry, painting, or song.
  • The world often watches this suffering with indifference, but on the ground, hope never dies.
  • International solidarity, the voices rising for justice, and the unshakable determination of the Palestinian people serve as reminders that as long as injustice exists, the struggle for freedom and dignity will continue.
  • Deep love and connection to the land across generations
